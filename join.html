Join Vahini – Admission & Contact

Whether you're discovering the elegance of Kuchipudi for the first time or wish to deepen your practice, Vahini Dance Academy opens its doors to passionate learners of all ages. We nurture every student’s artistic journey with personalized attention, a structured curriculum, and an environment rooted in tradition and discipline.

Admissions
Open Year-Round

We offer regular admissions across three learning levels:


Beginner (Ages 5+)

Introduction to basics of Kuchipudi

Body posture, hand gestures (mudras), footwork

Simple rhythm patterns and bhava (expression)

Interactive sessions to instill love for the art


Intermediate

Learning full jatis and simple varnams

Deeper focus on tala (rhythm), abhinaya, and balance

Introduction to performance-level compositions

Theory: deities, epics, classical dance history


Advanced (By Audition Only)

Mastery of varnams, tarangams, and padams

Personalized feedback and stage performance grooming

Choreography and lecture demonstration training

Exposure to inter-academy events and festivals
